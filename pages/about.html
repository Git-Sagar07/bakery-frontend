How It All Began 🌟

What started as a small kitchen experiment has blossomed into Pune's beloved bakery. In 2015, our founder, Priya Sharma, decided to turn her grandmother's secret recipes into something the whole city could enjoy.

From our humble beginnings with just one oven and a dream, we've grown into a community favorite, serving thousands of happy customers every month. But one thing hasn't changed — our commitment to quality, freshness, and that homemade taste that makes every bite special.
